Our Sarawakian Roots

Based in the heart of Kuching, The Obsidian Gallery is more than a breeder; we are custodians of the Betta hobby. Every specimen undergoes a 14-day strict conditioning process using native ketapang leaves and mineral-balanced Sarawakian water.

Our expertise stems from a decade of competing in national shows, ensuring that every "Living Jewel" you receive is a champion in spirit and health.
